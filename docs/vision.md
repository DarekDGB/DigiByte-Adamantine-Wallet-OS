# Adamantine Wallet — Vision

Status: draft v0.1

The Adamantine Wallet represents a next-generation DigiByte experience: ultra-secure, multi-layer, modular, and future‑proof.  
It is designed to unify payments, DigiAssets, messaging, analytics, and autonomous defense systems into a single trusted application.

## Core Vision

- Provide the safest and most intelligent DigiByte wallet ever built.
- Integrate all 5 security layers of your Quantum Shield, plus the Adaptive Immune Core.
- Enable seamless DigiAssets creation, transfer, and management.
- Support advanced communication using Enigmatic Layer‑0.
- Deliver consistent UX across iOS, Android, and Web.
- Maintain strict privacy, transparency, and full MIT‑licensed open-source freedom.

## What Adamantine Represents

- **Unbreakable trust** — powered by autonomous detection + self‑healing defense.
- **Total sovereignty** — users own their keys, their data, their identity.
- **Future readiness** — post‑quantum containers, DQSN, and adaptive learning.
- **Community empowerment** — DigiAssets revival, developer APIs, modular plug‑ins.

## Long‑Term Vision

- Become the official flagship wallet of DigiByte.
- Serve as the foundation for global DigiByte adoption.
- Provide tooling for builders, developers, and enterprises.
- Fully integrate with future DigiByte upgrades (Taproot+, Schnorr+, PQC).
